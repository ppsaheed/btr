What is an AMC?

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a pre-agreed service programme between you and Better Care, covering all scheduled maintenance visits, inspections, and priority support for your property's critical systems — across a defined period.

Rather than reacting to breakdowns, an AMC puts your property on a structured preventive care cycle — eliminating surprise failures, extending equipment life, and ensuring every system performs at its rated efficiency all year round.

Electrical Systems

Periodic inspection of wiring, distribution boards, MCBs, earthing systems, and emergency power circuits.

HVAC & Air Conditioning

Scheduled servicing of split, cassette, ducted, and central AC systems using advanced eco-safe technology.

Plumbing & Water Systems

Comprehensive care for all water supply, drainage, and tank systems. Includes bio-sanitary tank cleaning.
